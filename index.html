My Favorite Restaurants in Saipan

Saipan is considered to be a “melting pot” because of the diverse cultures of people who live here. Diverse cultures sprout many different types of dishes for everyone to try. These are my top 5 restaurants, with a brief explanation and my favorite dishes from them.

  1. J’s Restaurant
  2. New Seaside Restaurant
  3. Zoom Cafe
  4. Java Joes
