• J's Restaurant

J's Restaurant has two locations located in Gualo Rai and San Jose. My go to spot would be the one located in Gualo Rai, because of the long history of my family and I going there. My favorite meals I always order are the Cheeseburger with Fries and the Hamburger Steak. They also have a small arcade area for entertainment.

 

• New Seaside Restaurant

New Seaside Restaurant is located along beach road passing National Office Supply heading North. It was formerly known as "New Double Restaurant." My favorite dishes to get there are the Spicy Eggplant, Seweet and Sour Pork, and the Beef Broccoli. I recommend this restaurant to anyone looking for some Chinese cuisine.

 

• Zoom Cafe

Zoom Cafe is located in Susupe and is well known for their fried chickens. They have a variety of flavors of chicken, as well as other meals and drinks. I always order the Sweet and Sour or the Hot and Spicy flavored chicken. I also recommend the Caramel Frappe.

 

•Java Joes

Java Joes is located in Dan Dan and known for their famous pearl shakes, aside from the wide range of foods they serve. My favorite meal is the Cheeseburger with Fries, and I always order either a Java Chip or Caramel Shake with pearls on the side.

 

•Cup Bab

Cup Bab has two locations. One is located in San Jose, and is sort of an order-and-go type of restaurant. The other is located in Garapan, with a dine-in and drive-thru option available. My favorite meal is the Volcano Bab, which is a spicy pork bulgogi on top of rice and noodles.
